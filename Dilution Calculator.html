What is Meant by Dilution?

In Chemistry, dilution is a process in which the concentration of the solute in the solution is reduced. This process is carried out by usually adding more solvent. Usually, the acid or the base is mixed with the water to reduce the concentration of the ions in the solution. This process is called the dilution process. The process of dissolving acid or base with water is considered as a highly exothermic reaction.
