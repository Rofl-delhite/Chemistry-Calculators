What is Meant by Battery Life?

The battery life is the performance measure which is used to determine the lifetime of the battery or the longevity. A battery is an electronic device which can be charged using the electric device. A battery is a combination of two or more electrochemical cells. The batteries are classified as the primary battery and the secondary battery. The primary batteries are not rechargeable and it is considered as use and throw batteries. On the other hand, the secondary batteries are rechargeable and can be charged at any time.
