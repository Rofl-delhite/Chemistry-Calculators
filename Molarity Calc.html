What is Meant by the Molarity?

In Chemistry, the molarity is defined as the concentration of the solution. It is also known as the concentration of the chemical solution. The molarity of a chemical solution can be calculated by the number of moles of the solute per litre of the solution. The unit to indicate molarity is moles/litre. It is the most common unit to measure to determine the chemical solution concentration. The formula to molarity is given by
Molar Concentration, M = Number of Moles of Solute / Volume of the solution in litres.
