What is Meant by Moles to Grams?

In Chemistry, the moles to grams conversion represents the conversion of moles into grams. These measurements are used to determine the number of substances. To convert the moles into grams, multiply the mass of the substance by the molecular weight/ formula weight. In other words, it is the product of the mass of the substance and its molecular weight. Thus, the conversion of moles to grams formula is :
Grams = Mass of the substance (in moles) × Molecular weight
